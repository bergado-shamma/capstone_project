How to Log In to the Property and Facilities Reservation System (PFRS)

Follow these simple steps to log in and manage your reservations.

Step-by-Step Login Guide
  1. Enter Your Email Address:
    • Find the input field labeled "Email".
    • Type your registered email (e.g., mail@sample.com).
  2. Enter Your Password:
    • Locate the "Password" field.
    • Enter your secure password.
  3. Remember Me (Optional):
    • Check the "Remember Me" box to save login details.
  4. Click the Login Button:
    • Click the "Login" button to proceed.
Forgot Your Password?
  1. Click "Forgot Password?":
    • Click the link on the login page to open the reset pop-up.
  2. Enter Your Email:
    • Input your account’s email in the pop-up form.
  3. Click "Send":
    • The system sends a reset link to your email.
  4. Check Your Email:
    • Open your inbox and follow the reset instructions.
  5. Cancel (Optional):
    • Click "Cancel" to close the reset pop-up.

Need more help? Contact your system administrator or supervisor for assistance.
